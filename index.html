Katie Tubiello's How to Make a Tweet Image in Illustrator

An Instruction Challenge for JOUR108

How to Make an Awesome Tweet That Everyone Will Love

Assembly time: 30 minutes
Awesome rating: 45% awesome

Wondering how to spice up your Tweets? Don't know how to make your own images in Illustrator? Well you are in luck because this guide will take you step-by-step into making an awesome Image Tweet!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Laptop or other device
  • A Twitter account
  • Illustrator subscription (theres a 7 day free trial)
  • Your imagination
  • Optional: Photos

  1. Open Illustrator and make a new art board that is 1600 x 900 pixels.

    Make sure it is in RGB Mode.

    Proper Settings Set-Up.

  2. If doing a photo then go to add an image and add your image to the art board. Adjust height and width as needed to cover the art board.Go to step 4.

    If not using an image, go to the next step.

    Select Your Photo.

  3. If not doing an image, draw a box over the art board and change it to a color of your choosing.

    Choose a color that fits your theme.

    Place The Box Over The Art Board.

  4. Add a text box and change your font,size,and color of the text.

    Add in your text.

    Set-up Your Settings For Your Text.

  5. Move around the text and find a space that makes sense for your design.

    Find A Space That Fits.

  6. Once happy with all elements, click export and export to a PNG file.

    Save to photos.

    Export To A PNG.

  7. Start a new tweet and upload your photo.

    Upload Your Photo To Twitter.

  8. Write out your tweet, add hashtags, edit it and then click tweet!.

    Add Hastags and Write Out The Tweet. Hit Upload.

  9. YouTube Video
