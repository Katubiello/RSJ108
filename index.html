How to Make an Awesome Tweet That Everyone Will Love

Assembly time: 30 minutes
Awesome rating: 45% awesome

Wondering how to spice up your Tweets? How about not knowing how to make your own images in Illustrator? Well you are in luck because this guide will take you step-by-step into making an awesome Image Tweet!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Laptop or other device
  • A Twitter account
  • Illustrator subscription (theres a 7 day free trial)
  • Your imagination
  • Optional: Photos

  1. Open Illustrator and make a new art board that is 1600 x 900 pixels.

    Make sure it is in RGB Mode.

    Proper Settings Set-Up.

  2. If doing a photo, then go to "add an image" and add your image to the art board.

    Adjust height and width as needed to cover the art board. Go to step 4. If not using an image, go to step 3.

    a data-flickr-embed="true" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/198160791@N03/52834987466/in/dateposted-public/" title="D1EA118D-849B-4349-9C0F-3A22A0208AE2">

    Add the photo of your choosing.

  3. If not doing an image, draw a box over the art board and change it to a color of your choosing.

  4. Add a text box and change your font,size,and color of the text.

    Add in your text

    Text Box Personalization Settings.

  5. Move around the text and find a space that makes sense for your design.

  6. Once happy with all elements, click export and export to a PNG file.

    Save to photos.
